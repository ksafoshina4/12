Services

OJSC «Belgalantereya» offers the following services to wholesale customers:

- Provision of documents confirming the quality of goods

- Picking and packing small consignments

- Loading goods on buyers’ trucks

- Delivery of goods to the buyer by the transport of OJSC «Belgalantereya» in Minsk and Minsk region

- 

To all interested companies we offer:

- Complex storage services at the enterprise space at Minsk, Botanicheskaya Str. 5a

- Rent of offices and warehouse space at Botanicheskaya Str. 5a

- Transport services for delivery of goods on the territory of the Republic of Belarus

- replacement or refund of the defective goods.

Further information about custody and rent of premises is available by phone. 282-82-95
