About

 OJSC «Belgalantereya» is one of the oldest wholesale enterprises in the Republic of Belarus, which has its own rich history and tradition. It was founded in 1948 in Minsk.

Our company consistently works and performs one of the main functions, aimed at providing the population with a wide range of manufactured goods, namely synthetic detergents, household and toilet soap, household chemical goods, perfumes, cosmetics, linens, bedspreads, towels and other plastics and haberdashery.

Cooperating with more than 200 industrial enterprises of the Republic of Belarus and other suppliers, many of which have given us the right to be their official representatives, the company has gained a reputation as a reliable partner. This allows us to sell the goods to the trading enterprises at the prices of producers, which share in turnover makes 77%.

OJSC «Belgalantereya» supplies products to domestic and foreign markets, more than 1,000 market, non-market consumers and entrepreneurs. About 5,000 items of consumer goods are constantly available in stock.

In 2012 the company was awarded the "Bronze Mercury," an honorary diploma of the Ministry of Trade of the Republic of Belarus, for the high level of wholesale and retail trade of consumer goods.

OJSC «Belgalantereya» has offices and warehouses located in the center of Minsk, good material and technical base, allowing to provide legal entities and individuals with a range of services for storage and warehousing of goods, lease of premises and freight transporting.
