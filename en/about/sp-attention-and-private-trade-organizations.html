SP attention and private trade organizations

To the attention of individual entrepreneurs and private trading enterprises

Dear individual entrepreneurs!

OJSC “Belgalantereya” offers to private trading enterprises and individual entrepreneurs mutually beneficial cooperation in the matters of purchase of domestic and imported goods for cash and via bank transfer.

Benefits of cooperation with OJSC “Belgalantereya”:

- wide range of domestic and imported goods, including 77% of goods offered at the manufacturer’s prices without charging wholesale margin;

- permanent availability of domestic goods, included to the mandatory range of goods, approved by the Ministry of Trade of the Republic of Belarus;

- there is a cash and carry warehouse, located on the premises of OJSC “Belgalantereya” at the following address: 5a Botanicheskaya str., Minsk, with a possibility of payment for cash and via bank transfer and provision of the entire package of required documents;

- upon mutual agreement of the parties, the delivery of goods to the purchaser may be performed by transport vehicles of the enterprise in Minsk and Minsk Region.

For more complete satisfaction of Your needs in the imported goods and work activation, please fill out the questionary, which may be downloaded here.

You may leave an order for our products by one of the following ways:

1. Click the button “Download the pricelist” or in section “Goods”

- download the pricelist you need and save the file (format .xls).

- open the downloaded and saved file (Exel).

- enter the necessary amount of goods in column “Order”

- save changes in the file.

- send the order (this file) to the following email: This email address is being protected from spambots. You need JavaScript enabled to view it. or print it out and send by fax with a cover letter specifying your banking details and contact phone number.

2. Send an order for delivery of goods in free form by fax: +375 17 233-99-12, +375 17 294-04-00 or order goods through the button “Contact the marketing service (Place an order)”

3. Visit the office with samples of goods or the warehouse to select the goods personally

4. Call the numbers listed in section “Contacts”

After Your order has been received and processed, You will receive by fax or by email an invoice for (advance) payment or an order confirmation (deferred payment).

To enter into the delivery contract, You need to provide the following documents:

For residents of the Republic of Belarus

- notarized copy of State Registration Certificate;

- copy of Passport (upon presentation of the original);

- Power of Attorney relating to transaction (if You are the authorized representative of the business entity, on behalf of which the purchase of goods is performed).

For non-residents of the Republic of Belarus

- notarized copy of State Registration Certificate;

- notarized copy of Tax Authority Registration Certificate;

- copy of Passport (upon presentation of the original);

- Power of Attorney relating to transaction (if You are the authorized representative of the business entity, on behalf of which the purchase of goods is performed).

Further information on entering into agreements is available by the following phone number: +375 17 294-12-12

We are looking forward to effective cooperation.
