Store - Warehouse       

Warehouse store sells goods to legal entities, their separate divisions and to individual entrepreneurs in small lots for cash and cashless payment.

Warehouse store works by the method of self-service, with the right of buyers to select products from the place of storage.

Acceptance of cash by the warehouse store is performed by using cash registers or special computer systems.

Documentation of cash transactions is performed in accordance with the laws of the Republic of Belarus.

Sale of goods by the warehouse store is documented by consignment notes or waybills.

Individuals are not served by the warehouse store.
